About Us

Parnav Foundation is a dynamic non-governmental organization founded by a group of passionate engineers with a shared vision: to uplift the lives of those in need and empower them to secure an honest and dignified livelihood.
  • Vision
  • Mission
To be a beacon of hope and positive change, Parnav Foundation a world where every life is valued, every child has a bright future, and communities unite in times of need to build a better, more compassionate society.
At Parnav Foundation, our mission is to foster positive change, uplift communities, and contribute to the well-being of society through our diverse initiatives.

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Rapid Response and Relief

We are committed to swiftly responding to disasters and crises by providing essential relief and support to affected communities, aiding in their recovery and rebuilding efforts.

Promoting Lifesaving Act

We strive to encourage and facilitate voluntary blood donations through our Blood Donation Camps and awareness events, ensuring a stable and accessible blood supply for medical emergencies.

Empowering Every Child

Our objective is to provide a nurturing environment for orphaned and underprivileged children through adoption and education, enabling them to develop into confident, capable individuals ready to make a difference in the world.

Community Engagement

We aim to engage and empower local communities through education, awareness, and collaboration, creating a network of individuals dedicated to positive change
